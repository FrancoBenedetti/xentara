# Project Naming & Brand Identity

The **Xentara Framework** (formerly Lume Centrix) is designed to transform chaotic data into curated community intelligence using an autonomous agentic pipeline.

The name **Xentara** was chosen to represent a modern, premium, and multi-tenant "Platform-as-a-Framework" for Community Intelligence Hubs.

---

### Previous Naming Directions (for context)

Here were the initial naming directions based on the framework's unique "ACAF" (Agentic Content-Aggregation Framework) identity:

### 1. Conceptual & Abstract (Focus on "Light" and "Clarity")

* **LucidNode**: Suggests clarity and a point of connection.
* **Aetheris**: Evokes a refined, high-level atmospheric layer.
* **PrismHub**: Reflects the "filtering" and "refining" process.
* **Clearsight**: Directly addresses the "anti-chaos" value proposition.

### 2. Architectural & Technical (Focus on "Agentic" and "Framework")

* **AxiomAgent**: Suggests a foundational truth or "Core" logic.
* **SynapseLayer**: Highlights the AI-driven "brain" behind the content.
* **NeuralVault**: Focuses on the "Taste Graph" and distilled intelligence.
* **AgenticCore**: Simple, descriptive, and highlights the "Platform-as-a-Framework" nature.

### 3. Community & Connection (Focus on "Hubs" and "Niches")

* **CollectiveIQ**: Emphasizes the "Collective Intelligence Loop."
* **KindredHub**: Focuses on the "Niche Audience" and high-trust environment.
* **SphereLink**: Suggests an isolated, secure environment (Hub/Workspace).
* **CommuneAI**: A portmanteau of "Community" and "AI."

### 4. Evocative & Premium (Modern SaaS Branding)

* **Vantage**: Suggests a better perspective or "viewing" point.
* **Origin**: Suggests the starting point of truth/content.
* **Arcane**: (Used in a modern sense) suggests a high-level, sophisticated system.
* **Sentio**: (Latin for "to feel/perceive") reflects the "Sentiment Tracking" agents.

---

### Chosen Name: **Xentara**

The brand **Xentara** conveys a sense of "Centricity" (from the original Centrix) combined with a "X" factor representing the advanced agentic intelligence layer. It positions the platform as the definitive "Hub" for specialized community intelligence.
