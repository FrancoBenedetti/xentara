# Xentara Curator User Experience: Sequence & Branches

This document outlines the end-to-end journey for a primary Xentara Hub Curator, from initialization to audience engagement.

## 1. Hub Foundation & Identity
1.  **Account Initialization**: Curator logs into the Xentara Dashboard.
2.  **Hub Creation**: Defines Hub Name, Slug, and Core Identity.
3.  **Visual DNA (Branding)**: Sets brand colors, Logo URL, and general aesthetic guidelines.
4.  **Intelligence Scoping (Lenses)**: Defines the initial "Flavors" (confirmed tags) and sets the **Strictness Mode** (Exploratory vs. Strict).

---

## 2. Source Ingestion & Discovery
1.  **Source Entry**: Curator adds whitelisted URLs (Youtube Channels, RSS Feeds).
2.  **Discovery Trigger**: Service Layer initiates the "Discovery Agent."
3.  **Initial Triage**: The curator views "Raw" content as it flows into the dashboard.

---

## 3. The Intelligence Loop (Refinement)
1.  **AI Analysis**: The Curator reviews AI-generated summaries, sentiment scores, and suggested bylines.
2.  **Taxonomy Stewardship (Branching point)**:
    *   **Branch A (Confirmation)**: Curator approves AI-suggested taxonomy "Drafts."
    *   **Branch B (Refinement)**: Curator edits descriptions of suggested flavors to better align with the Hub's voice.
    *   **Branch C (Merging)**: Curator merges similar suggestions into existing confirmed lenses.

---

## 4. Publication & Distribution
1.  **Selection**: Curator identifies "In-Focus" publications ready for the audience.
2.  **Formatting (Multi-Medium Strategy)**:
    *   Curator engages the **AI Formatting Assistant** to convert the publication card into an optimized plain-text format for Telegram or WhatsApp.
3.  **Delivery Action**: 
    *   **Action A (Instant Push)**: Curator triggers a selective push to connected messenger channels.
    *   **Action B (Client Feature)**: Curator "pins" or features the article on the PWA Client.
4.  **Republishing Loop**:
    *   Curator views the **Publication History** (Log of timestamps and media).
    *   Curator chooses to **Republish** an older item with "Fresh Commentary" or updated context.

---

## 5. Audience Engagement & Analytics
1.  **Engagement Review**: Curator monitors real-time stats (reactions, read counts, shares).
2.  **Feedback Analysis**: Curator reads anonymous/identified consumer responses.
3.  **Consumer Insights**: Curator views the "Enrolled Consumers" list and identifies power-users or community trends.

---

## 6. Management & Scaling
1.  **Team Expansion**: Curator invites additional users (Editors/Maintainers) via RBAC settings.
2.  **Hub Discovery (Future)**: Curator lists the Hub on the **Global Subscription Client** to attract new subscribers from the broader Xentara ecosystem.
